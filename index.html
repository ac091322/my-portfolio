About Me



I've always been drawn to structure—seeking stability and consistency in my work and the products I produce. Whether it's coding (which I'm excited to pursue as my future, full-time career), translating documents during my localization roles from December 2021 to January 2024, or wireframing projects as a project manager for over seven years, I strive for a clear, organized format that brings coherence to the final result. This focus isn't about following rigid rules but ensuring each project maintains its own internal logic and clarity.

Coding, in particular, appeals to my need to be pixel-perfect, where even a single punctuation mark or extra space matters (depending on the language). Whether it's a Canva logo, HTML file, or wireframe, I want the spacing on the right side to be as precise as the left; I want the gaps and padding to be exact. This meticulous approach fuels my excitement for coding as a way to create scalable, structured, and high-performance solutions.

I'm eager to leverage my attention to detail and problem-solving skills within a team. I've gained hands-on experience coding in JavaScript and Python, using tools like React for dynamic UI development and Redux for state management. I particularly enjoy React's ability to update pages without a hard refresh and the use of ternary operators and short-circuit evaluations to dynamically render HTML elements. Python, however, remains a favorite for its power, elegance, and readability—its structured syntax and enforced indentation make code clean and organized, which is why I especially appreciate features like list comprehensions and generator expressions for their conciseness and readability—what a clean way to write code! On the backend, I enjoy using both Express and Flask to build RESTful APIs. Their simple, flexible routing systems enable the fast development of scalable, efficient services that integrate seamlessly with front-end applications and third-party tools. I can't wait to contribute to meaningful projects and bring collaborative efforts to life.

Outside of work, I love traveling and discovering new places. In my free time, I explore destinations and capture the beauty of nature, cityscapes, and architecture with my phone. Memorable places include Hokkaido, Amsterdam, Ibiza, Paris, Bangkok, Taipei, and Angkor Wat.

The more I learn, the more I realize how much I don't know!
